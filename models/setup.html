Setup Guide

Skterabytes TV Setup & Installation

Your step-by-step guide to unboxing, setting up, and enjoying your new Skterabytes television

Prajin Sivakumar

Product Specialist at Skterabytes

Congratulations on your new Skterabytes TV! This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything from unboxing to advanced setup options. Whether you've purchased our flagship QLED model or one of our premium OLED displays, these instructions will help you get the most out of your television.

Before You Begin

  • Estimated setup time: 15-30 minutes
  • Tools needed: None (everything included)
  • Ensure you have a stable Wi-Fi connection
  • Keep your remote control batteries ready
1

Unboxing Your Skterabytes TV

Let's start by safely unboxing your television and identifying all components.

What's in the Box:

  • Skterabytes Television - Carefully wrapped in protective foam
  • Stand Base & Supports - For tabletop placement
  • Remote Control - With batteries included
  • Power Cable - Country-specific plug
  • Quick Start Guide - Basic setup instructions
  • Warranty Information - Your protection plan

Safety Tip

Always unbox your TV on a soft, flat surface. For screens larger than 55", we recommend having a second person assist with lifting.

2

Assembling the Stand

Choose between wall mounting (bracket not included) or using the included stand.

Tabletop Installation

  1. Place the stand base on a flat, stable surface
  2. Align the support arms with the TV's VESA mounting points
  3. Secure with the provided screws (tool-less on most models)
  4. Ensure all connections are tight before lifting

Wall Mounting

  1. Use a VESA-compatible mount (sold separately)
  2. Follow the mount manufacturer's instructions
  3. Ensure wall studs can support the TV's weight
  4. Consider professional installation for larger models

Recommended Placement

  • Eye level should be at 1/3 from the bottom of the screen for optimal viewing
  • Maintain at least 4 inches of clearance on all sides for ventilation
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent glare and heat buildup
3

Initial Power Up & Setup

Let's bring your TV to life and walk through the initial configuration.

First-Time Setup Process

  1. Connect Power

    Plug in the power cable to both the TV and wall outlet. The Skterabytes logo should light up.

  2. Remote Setup

    Insert the included batteries into your remote (positive ends facing out).

  3. Language Selection

    Use the remote to select your preferred language from the on-screen menu.

  4. Wi-Fi Connection

    Choose your network and enter the password. Ethernet is also available.

  5. Software Update

    We recommend installing the latest updates for optimal performance.

Pro Tip

During setup, enable "Quick Start" mode in settings for faster boot times. This uses slightly more standby power but significantly improves responsiveness.
